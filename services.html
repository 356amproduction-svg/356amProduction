Terms & Agreement

Please read and agree to the following before submitting your booking request.

1. RAW Image Files

3:56am Production does not deliver RAW image files under any circumstances. All final deliverables are fully edited JPEG or digital image files. RAW files are proprietary to the photographer and will not be shared, transferred, or sold to the client at any time.

2. What the Photographer Is Not Responsible For

3:56am Production is not responsible for the following:

  • Unfavorable weather conditions, natural lighting limitations, or environmental factors beyond the photographer's control on the day of the session.
  • Location unavailability, permit denials, or restricted access to a requested shooting location.
  • Missed moments caused by late arrival, uncooperative subjects, or failure by the client to communicate key shot requests in advance.
  • Loss or damage of images due to memory card failure, equipment malfunction, or unforeseen technical failure beyond the photographer's control. In such cases, a reshoot may be offered at the photographer's discretion.
  • Third-party interference, uninvited guests disrupting the session, or distractions that affect the quality of the final images.
  • Client dissatisfaction resulting from poses, expressions, or styling choices made by the client during the session.
  • Any health, safety, or legal issues that arise at the chosen location.

3. Image Delivery & Editing

All delivered images include professional color grading and standard editing. Skin retouching is only included if selected as an add-on. The photographer retains full creative discretion over the editing style and final selection of delivered images.

4. Deposit & Payment Policy

A 30% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your session date. This deposit is due at the time of booking confirmation and goes toward your total session balance. The remaining balance is due on or before the session date.

  • The deposit is non-refundable once the session has been booked and confirmed by the photographer.
  • If the client cancels or reschedules, the deposit is forfeited and a new deposit will be required to rebook.
  • Exception: If 3:56am Production must cancel your session due to a photographer family emergency, your deposit will be refunded in full within 5–7 business days.

5. Cancellations & Rescheduling

Session changes must be communicated at least 48 hours in advance. Last-minute cancellations may result in a rebooking fee. Final pricing and deposit terms will be confirmed during your consultation call.

6. Copyright

3:56am Production retains full copyright of all images captured during the session. The client is granted a personal use license for the delivered images. Images may be used by 3:56am Production for portfolio, social media, and promotional purposes unless the client requests otherwise in writing.
